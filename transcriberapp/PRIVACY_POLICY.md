# Privacy Policy for Audio Transcriber

**Last Updated: December 8, 2025**

## Introduction

Audio Transcriber ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Audio Files
- Audio files you share with the app or record using the app
- Audio files are temporarily uploaded to our server for transcription processing
- Audio files are NOT permanently stored on our servers
- Audio files and transcriptions are stored locally on your device

### Transcription Data
- Text transcriptions of your audio files
- Stored locally in your device's private storage
- We do not have access to your stored transcriptions

### Technical Information
- Device model and Android version (for app functionality)
- App crash logs (if you opt-in to crash reporting)

## How We Use Your Information

- **Audio Processing**: Audio files are sent to our server which uses Google's Gemini AI API to generate transcriptions
- **Local Storage**: Transcriptions and audio files are stored only on your device
- **No Selling**: We DO NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:
- **Google Gemini AI**: For audio transcription processing. Please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Data Security

- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Audio files are deleted from our server immediately after transcription
- Your device data is stored in the app's private storage, inaccessible to other apps

## Data Retention

- **On Device**: Audio files and transcriptions remain on your device until you delete them
- **On Server**: Audio files are deleted immediately after processing (typically within seconds)
- **Uninstall**: All app data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete individual recordings and transcriptions within the app
- Uninstall the app to remove all data
- Contact us for any privacy concerns

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
